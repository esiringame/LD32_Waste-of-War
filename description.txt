Synopsis:
In a small village located in the desert, children are sent to collect water from a distant well. However, former wars left a merciless minefield in between.
Lead their steps through the minefield and bring the precious liquid to your thirsty people.

Controls:
Arrow keys: move
Spacebar: jump
Left-click: throw a stone or leave it at your foot

Mines:
Red mine explodes when you leave it. Remember to put a stone on it. 
Green mine explodes briefly after you walk on it. Jump quickly.

You need the sound on to play the game.
